The Graphics Processing Unit (CPU)

The GPU will handle the generation of the picture you see on your screen

There are several cores within the GPU that will help with multiple processing

Can be great for video games, rendering software, watching 4k videos, etc

There are currently two main companies that design video cards: Nvidia and AMD

Of those two brands, there are many companies that create these video cards (MSI, Gigabyte, Sapphire, etc...)

These can get quite expensive depending on what you want to use it for.

The speed is often measured in Flops
